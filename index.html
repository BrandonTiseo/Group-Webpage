Our Mission Statement

As Software Developers at the Chum Bucket we strive to constantly innovate and improve the customer experience. As developers we want to create efficient and polished products that push the boundary of what is possible. We always aim to create a welcoming and productive team environment in order to meet the above goals.

Our Team Rules:

  • All team members must actively participate in meetings, discussions, and assigned tasks.
  • Tasks should be completed on time, and members must communicate any challenges they face.
  • Team members should speak openly about project challenges and propose solutions.
  • Constructive feedback is encouraged to improve the project continuously.
  • Team members should concentrate on the Sprint objectives and avoid unnecessary distractions.
  • Sprint goals should be clearly defined to ensure alignment and productivity.
  • Daily or weekly stand-up meetings will be conducted to discuss progress and obstacles.
  • Team members should acknowledge and respect each other's strengths and weaknesses.
